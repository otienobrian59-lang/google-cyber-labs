## Notes
The NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF) consists of seven steps:

1. **Prepare**: Establish governance, assign roles, and identify system boundaries.
2. **Categorize**: Categorize systems based on the impact of potential breaches (low, moderate, or high).
3. **Select**: Choose appropriate security controls from NIST SP 800-53 based on categorization.
4. **Implement**: Put the selected security controls into practice.
5. **Assess**: Test controls to confirm their effectiveness.
6. **Authorize**: Decision by an authorizing official on whether the system can operate.
7. **Monitor**: Continuous monitoring of control implementation and risks.
